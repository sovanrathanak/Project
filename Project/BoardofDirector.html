Board of Director

Mr. Kim Youn Soo

Chairman

Mr. Kim Youn Soo was appointed as Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Company in August 2018. Currently, he is the General Manager of Global Business Department of NongHyup Bank. Mr. Kim Youn Soo spent many years with the National Agriculture Cooperative Federation (NACF). His latest position with NACF was the General Manager of the Office of the Board of Directors. Mr. Kim Youn Soo holds a BA in German Education from Chung Ang University and is bilingual in Japanese and English.

Mr. Seo Joon Yong

CEO(Member)

Mr. Seo Joon Yong was appointed as a member of the Board of Directors of the Company in August 2018. Prior to his appointment, he held various management positions with NongHyup Bank in the Republic of Korea over 14 years. One of his recent positions was Team Leader of Investment Banking Department managing three important investment projects - Gaziantep Health Campus Project in Turkey worth USD40,000,000 in 2016, LNG Plant Project in Sulawesi, Indonesia, worth USD30,000,000 in 2015, and Steel Mill Construction Project in Brazil worth USD50,000,000 in 2014. Mr. Seo obtained a BA in English Literature from Chung Ang University in 1987. In addition, he became a Certified Financial Planner in 2004 and a Certified Credit Analyst in 2006 (Korea Banking Institute). He also attended Accounting and Management Programs of Korea Accounting and Information School in 2008 and Advanced Banking Program of Seoul National University in 2012.

Mr. Song Kosal

Independent Member

Mr. Song Kosal was appointed as an Independent Member of the Board of Directors of the Company in August 2018. He has been a Tax Lecturer at CamEd Business School since 2012. He has over 20 year experience in tax and management consulting. This includes providing advice to businesses in relation to taxation and investment issues in Cambodia, involving in setting up a strategy for business development, leading teams to conduct tax audit and tax health check for companies, and liaising with government officers. Before becoming a member of the Board of Directors of NongHyup Finance Plc, he worked for CamTB Trusted Solutions as Managing Director, Ernst & Young (Cambodia) as Tax Director, Bun & Associates as Tax Deputy Head, KPMG as Tax and Corporate Services Supervisor, and PwC as Senior Tax Consultant. Mr. Song Kosal holds a BA in Accounting from the National Institute of Management, Cambodia and a Master of Economics from Nagoya University, Japan. In 2009, he received a Certificate of Doctoral Course Completion from Nagoya University, Japan. He is also bilingual in Japanese and English.
