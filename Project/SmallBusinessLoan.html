Small Business Loan (USD 3,001 – USD 30,000)

The purpose of this small business loan is to help clients initiate new businesses and/or expand their existing ones for more profitability with sustainable growth. This loan product is provided to clients – in both rural and urban areas – who have their regular micro/small businesses in several sectors. It is provided in two currencies – Khmer Riel and US Dollars.​​

Products Features

  • Loan size:Up to USD 3,000 / KHR 12,000,000
  • Interest rate:Reasonable rate
  • Loan term:Up to 36 Months
  • Collateral:Hard or soft title
  • Repayment mode:Flexible

Benefits

  • Reasonable interest rate with appropriate loan term
  • Repayment methodology based on customer’s business income
  • Fast services and friendly staff
  • Repayment is available at branch office, clients’ home or business partners (Wing / True Money)

Requirements

  • Be a maturity aged 18 to 65 years old
  • Permanent resident in NongHyup’s operation areas
  • Have a legal business and regular income
  • Have a proper collateral
  • Visit operation areas of NongHyup Finance
