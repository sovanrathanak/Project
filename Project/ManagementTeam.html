Management Team

Mr. Seo Joonyong

Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Seo was appointed as Chief Executive Officer of the Company in August 2018. Prior to his appointment, he held various management positions with NongHyup Bank in the Republic of Korea over 14 years. One of his recent positions was Team Leader of Investment Banking Department managing three important investment projects - Gaziantep Health Campus Project in Turkey worth USD40,000,000 in 2016, LNG Plant Project in Sulawesi, Indonesia, worth USD30,000,000 in 2015, and Steel Mill Construction Project in Brazil worth USD50,000,000 in 2014. Mr. Seo obtained a BA in English Literature from Chung Ang University in 1987. In addition, he became a Certified Financial Planner in 2004 and a Certified Credit Analyst in 2006 (Korea Banking Institute). He also attended Accounting and Management Programs of Korea Accounting and Information School in 2008 and Advanced Banking Program of Seoul National University in 2012.

Mrs. CHHEAV Kimna

Company Secretary

She has played critical roles in various secretarial works of the Institution. Kimna used to work with various international organizations including FIDR as Project Assistant and CCF as Administrator and Finance Assistant. She has over 10 years of extensive experience in administrative and secretarial works. Kimna is currently holding two bachelor degrees in Economics from the Royal University of Law and Economics and in English Literacy from Build Bright University. Furthermore, she has attended various training courses pertaining to her job duties.

Mr. OR Narin

Senior HR and Admin Manager

Was appointed as HR and Admin Manager in 2009 and Senior HR and Admin in January 2017. He served as Company Secretary and Legal Manager from October 2014 to May 2016. Since May 2016, he has resumed his position as HR and Administration Manager. Narin has developed a strong leadership and motivation skills through his many years’ experience in management and administration with various international organizations and private companies in Cambodia. During that time, he held a number of key positions including General Manager of Laser World Solutions, Project Marketing Manager of International Project Marketing, Sales Manager of InterBev, Personnel Manager of Power Networks International, and the Project Assistant of Rural Development Project. Mr. Narin holds an MBA in General Management from the Royal University of Law and Economics and a BBA in Business Administration from the National University of Management.

Mr. SA Sok

Finance Manager

Was appointed as Finance Manager in 2014, then as a Senior Finance Manager in January, 2018. Mr. Sok has more than 10 years of work experience in financial sector. His previous positions included Promotion and Underwriting Officer at CHC - MEADA, General Trainer at Credit Mutuel du Kampuchea (CMK), and Teacher of English at Asia HRDC. Sok holds an MBA in Finance and Banking and a BBA in Mathematics. He is currently pursuing studying toward an MBA in Organization Development specializing in Microfinance Management with Southeast Asia Interdisciplinary Development Institute (SAIDI), the Philippines.

Mr. HONG Ratha

ICT Manager

Ratha joined the Company as ICT Manager in May 2016. Before joining the Company, he was Management Information System Manager and Data Warehouse Team Leader of a well-known deposit-taking microfinance institution for more than 2 years. Ratha has over 13 years’ experience in IT administration, development and core banking system support in the financial and technological industries. Ratha holds a Bachelor of Management Information System from SETEC Institute. In addition, to strengthen his knowledge, he has attended some professional training courses such as Project Management, Team Building, Essential Communications, Empowering Leadership, Advanced Financial Management and other IT skills.

Mr. CHEA Pheng

Loan Recovery Manager

Was appointed as Loan Recovery Manager of NongHyup Finance (Cambodia) Plc in January 2019. Before joining NongHyup Finance, he worked as Head of Operations Department and, then, as Head of Training Department for a medium-sized microfinance institution in Cambodia. Pheng had been working with the same institution, which started as an NGO and later developed into an MFI for over 16 years. He started his first career as Community Bank Assistant from 1999 to 2000 and then, as Credit Officer from 2003 to 2006. Over the 9-year period from 2007-2015, he had been promoted as Portfolio Officer, Assistant to Operations Manager, Regional Manager, Head of Operations Department, and Head of Training Department with the same MFI. Mr. Pheng obtained his BBA in Banking and Finance from Build Bright University in 2009. He obtained his MBA in Management from the same university in 2011. In addition, he has attended various training courses on microfinance operations and management.

Mr. PEN Sovath

Internal Audit Manager

He has almost 10 years’ experience with Microfinance Sector. His previous positions included MIS Unit Manager, Compliance Unit Manager, Deputy Internal Audit and Risk Manager and then, he was promoted to Internal and Risk Manager in January 2016. Since October 2016, he has been appointed as an Internal Audit Manager. Before joining NongHyup Finance, he had worked as a Finance Manager of Cambodian Health Committee (CHC) from 2006 to 2012. Sovath holds BBA and MBA of Finance and Banking from Build Bright University. He also holds a Diploma in Accounting from Regent College, Cambodia. In addition, he has attended various trainings related to management and leadership.

Mr. PEL Chivita

Operations Manager

Was appointed as Operations Manager of NongHyup Finance (Cambodia) Plc in January 2019. Before joining NongHyup Finance, he used to hold various positions with a number of well-known organizations such as HKI, FAO, GRET and CEDAC as Program Coordinator, National Agriculture Advisor, Project Manager and Farmer Community Facilitator, respectively. Chivita has 14 years of extensive experience in managing projects relevant to agriculture, rural development, market linkage, agricultural value chains, agriculture cooperative, agriculture credit, marketing and research. Chivita holds a Master Degree of Business Administration specialized in Agriculture and Rural Development from Royal University of Agriculture (RUA) of Cambodia. He also holds a Bachelor Degree in Agriculture specialized in Agronomy Science from Preak Leap National College of Agriculture. Chivita attended various exposure visits and trainings in the related fields including the topics on voucher schemes and agricultural inputs, village and farm diagnosis, participatory learning and action for mutual self-empowerment, agriculture cooperative, microfinance management and product development for banking and financial services.

Mr. CHEA Sopharith

Risk Manager

Mr. UM Samnang

Marketing and Projects Manager
